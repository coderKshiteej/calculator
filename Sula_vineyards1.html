Sula vineyards

Sula Vineyards is one of the most famous and popular tourist destinations in Nashik. It is a leading winery in India, known for its exquisite wines and stunning vineyard landscapes. Here, visitors can indulge in wine tastings, guided tours, and learn about the winemaking process. The vineyards offer a serene and picturesque setting, making it a perfect spot for relaxation, leisurely walks, and enjoying the natural beauty. Sula Vineyards also hosts events and festivals, such as the SulaFest, which attracts music lovers from around the world. It is a must-visit place for wine enthusiasts, nature lovers, and those seeking a unique and memorable experience in Nashik.Sula Classics Sula’s wine offering includes over 30 variants[6] - Sula Brut - matured for 2–4 months. It contains an ABV of 12%. Sula Brut Tropicale - Sula’s first Blanc de Noirs is a blend of 60% Chenin Blanc and 40% includes Shiraz and Riesling. It is bottle-aged for 18 months and contains an ABV of 12%. Sula Sparkling Shiraz - Sula Sparkling Shiraz is India’s first and only sparkling shiraz wine. Matured for 4–5 months in oak barrels, this wine contains an ABV of 13.5%. Sula Zinfandel Rosé - India’s first ever Zinfandel, this wine contains an ABV of 11.5%. Sula Riesling - This fruity white wine is India’s first Riesling and contains an ABV of 11%. Sula Sauvignon Blanc - India’s first ever Sauvignon Blanc, this unbaked white wine contains an ABV of 12.5%. Sula Chenin Blanc - This semi-dry white wine is India’s first ever Chenin Blanc as well as the country’s best-selling white wine. It contains an ABV of 12%.
